Job Portal System

The Job Portal Website project aims to create a platform where job seekers can search and apply for job opportunities, and employers can post job openings and search for suitable candidates. The website will provide a user-friendly interface that simplifies the job search process for both employers and job seekers. The Job Portal Website project aims to solve the problem of unemployment and the difficulty faced by job seekers in finding suitable job opportunities. Employers also face challenges in finding suitable candidates for their job openings. The Job Portal Website is a platform that connects job seekers and employers.

HOME page :

A user gets a various options on the home page . If the user is applicant then user can log in , Search a job by categories , Reach out to companies, Go to Profile section. If the user is Employer the he can do log in, Job posting .

Category wise job Page :

In this page applicant can search a job by category. As it saves time of applicant for searching a job from list of jobs. Applicant can choose their prior job category, location and search a job accordingly.
